Continuing Education Transcript System

The USC Rossier School of Education (RSOE) Continuing Education Transcript System (CETS) provides electronic tools for tracking continuing education students and printing transcripts at USC's education school.

Core features:
  • Tracks multiple student profiles;
  • Tracks multiple professional development courses;
  • Tracks transcript printing and fee history;
  • Quickly search for students while easily mixing and matching selection criteria;
  • Prints custom formatted transcripts; and,
  • Imports data from RegOnline with one click.
