Droptopia (offline)

Droptopia makes it easy to find a great Drupal shop by budget and location. Inspired by 37 Signal's excellent Sortfolio, Droptopia highlights firms that have Drupal expertise.

Built entirely in Drupal, the site uses Views, CCK, and a custom module to provide a simple interface to the user. Statik provided the site design.

Successfully launched in March, 2011.

  • Community manager
  • Technical architect

Core technologies:
