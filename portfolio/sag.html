SAG Foundation Drupal Site

The goal of the SAG Foundation is to "enhance the lives of actors by investing in programs which help them in their professional endeavors and the communities in which they live."

A new site built on Drupal helps further this mission by providing news ways to interact with and leverage the Foundation's large membership community, providing robust and complex events management and additional back office tools like volunteer tracking.

My role:

  • Project Manager
  • Client Manager
  • Technical architect

Successfully launched:

  • Client relationship continues after launch for new projects and advice
  • Glowing client referral to prospective client

Core features:

  • Fully featured events management system
  • Complex eligibility status tracking for individual members (based on attendance over time)
  • A community of over 15,000 working male and female actors
  • Full integration with the MailChimp API for composing and sending email newsletters to members and individual subscription management for members, all without leaving Drupal
  • Fully customized member home page for tracking RSVP history, eligibility status and upcoming events

Core technologies:

  • PHP 5, MySQL
  • Drupal
  • MailChimp
  • Google Maps
